This section of the questionnaire will help you discover where your digestive system is having problems. It is a screening tool and does not constitute an exact diagnosis of your problem. 0 = Symptom is not present/rarely present
1 = Mild/sometimes
2 = Moderate/often
3 = Severe/almost always
